The Aim

To create a free, simple and effective way to plan and save towards a specific event by using a calculator of earnings against expenses and other savings

This diagram represents the basic principle of 'Simple Stash' - to organise your money and the most significant part - "save".
Our aim is to to simplify your savings by providing a "personally affordable" savings amount each month to psychologically remove "save" from the diagram to make you feel like you are just working and enjoying.
